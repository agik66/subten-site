Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Summary: Your data is yours. We do not sell it, share it for marketing purposes, or use it for anything beyond providing the Subten app experience.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Subten (hereinafter "we" or "the app"). You can reach us at: privacy@subten-app.eu.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data Provided by the User

  • Registration data: email address, name (optional), age, sex, weight, height
  • Nutrition data: food logs, food photos, barcode scans, and nutrition label scans
  • Training data: workout logs, personal records, cardio activities
  • Progress data: body measurements (10 metrics), progress photos
  • AI Coach communication: messages and questions entered in the AI chat interface

2.2 Apple HealthKit Data

With the user's consent, we access data from Apple HealthKit including steps, heart rate, active energy, workouts, and weight. HealthKit data is never sold, shared with third parties for marketing, or used for advertising. This complies with Apple's HealthKit usage requirements.

2.3 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device type, iOS version
  • Language settings
  • Anonymized app usage analytics

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide app services (nutrition, training, and progress tracking)
  • To personalize AI coaching and recommendations
  • To calculate nutritional values and training statistics
  • To sync data via iCloud (if enabled)
  • To improve service quality (anonymized analytics only)

4. AI Processing and OpenRouter API

Subten uses artificial intelligence to provide coaching, analyze food from photos, and offer personalized recommendations. AI features are powered by Claude from Anthropic, accessed through the OpenRouter service.

BYOK (Bring Your Own Key): Premium users can use their own OpenRouter API key. In that case, API calls are billed directly to your OpenRouter account. Your API key is stored locally on your device and encrypted using the iOS Keychain. We never send it to our servers.

Data sent for AI processing:

  • Text messages in the AI chat interface
  • Food photos (for recognition)
  • Summary of daily nutritional data (for personalized advice)

This data is processed in real time and is not permanently stored on third-party servers. Anthropic does not use data sent through the API to train its models.
